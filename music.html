My Music

My dad got me my first guitar in high school and I fell in love with writing songs. I've always loved writing poems my whole life so lyrics came naturally. I've written over a hundred songs on at least 12 cds between then and the time I was 30. Here are a few of my favorites. I never quite made it to be a superstar. Not all dreams come true.
